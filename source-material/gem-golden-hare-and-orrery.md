## The Orrery of Los Feliz
(`The Orrery of Los Feliz.doc`)

> We were tipped off by a news item coupled with the creditors' lawsuits against Peter Hight, our
> synchronicity sensitives finding a common link between the two. The news item said that the FCC
> was investigating a pirate radio station somewhere in the Los Feliz area.

Full 260-word piece: investigators track down a warehouse in Los Feliz containing an enormous
(30-foot) working orrery — a moving model of the solar system topped with a live radio telescope —
built, mysteriously, by an unlikely community-college dropout. Complete noir-flavored sci-fi
vignette, distinct in tone from everything else in the archive (closest thing to "genre short
fiction" that isn't a screenplay).

(nebula.js, egg.js) given the literal solar-system subject matter.
